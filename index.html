    Who is King Pudding?

    King Pudding is a mascot character from the mobile game IDOLiSH7.

    What is IDOLiSH7?

    IDOLiSH7 is a Japanese rythmn game developed by Bandai Namco.

    Game

    The rythmn game consists of 4 areas to tap. There are normal taps, swipes, and holds. The story component is a visual novel style game about a group of 7 idols trying to reach the top with you as their manager. They encounter many challenges along the way, including some very unexpected violence.

    King Pudding's Role

    King Pudding is one of the mascots in the game. He appears on many event banners and other parts of the game.

    Cards

    King Pudding cards can be obtained from events or the shop.

    Abilities

    King Pudding cards allow you to level up your team.

    Special Card

    The special SSR card allows you to level up the special skills of your team.

    Autoplay

    Pudding bottles allow you to autoplay a song that you've achieved a rank of A or higher in.

    Anime

    IDOLiSH7 has an anime adaptation based on the story component of the game. There are currently 3 seasons. A 4th one has been announced.

    King Pudding's Role

    King Pudding is a celebrity in the IDOLiSH7 universe. He appears on products such as King Pudding, a pudding brand. Yotsuba Tamaki, one of the members of IDOLiSH7, is obsessed with King Pudding. He loves to eat King Pudding and has merchandise like plushies and posters of him.

    Backstory

    King Pudding has a sad backstory. He turned expressionless from the shock of being abandoned as a kid. Then he turned into a pudding when his current family took him in.
